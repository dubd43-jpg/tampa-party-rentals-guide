Water Slide Rentals in Tampa, FL

Florida summers call for water slides. Whether you're throwing a backyard birthday bash, a school end-of-year party, or a neighborhood block party, renting an inflatable water slide in Tampa is one of the best ways to beat the heat and keep guests of all ages entertained.

Types of Water Slide Rentals Available in Tampa

  • Single-lane water slides: Perfect for smaller backyards. These are tall, steep slides with a splash pool at the bottom — great for kids 5 and up.
  • Dual-lane racing slides: Two side-by-side lanes let kids race each other down. A crowd favorite at birthday parties and school events.
  • Wet/dry combo units: These combine a bounce area, climbing wall, and water slide in one inflatable. Maximum fun per square foot.
  • Waterslide obstacle courses: Long inflatable obstacles with water features throughout — perfect for older kids and teens.
  • Dunk tanks: A classic Tampa party addition. Fill it with water and let kids (and adults) soak the guest of honor.

What You Need for a Water Slide Rental in Tampa

Before booking, make sure you have:

  • A flat outdoor area at least 20x30 feet (most water slides need space plus a safety perimeter)
  • Access to a water hose and outdoor spigot within 50 feet of setup location
  • A standard 110v outdoor power outlet for the blower motor
  • Permission from your HOA if applicable
