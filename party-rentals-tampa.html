Party Rentals in Tampa, FL

Tampa is one of the most active cities in Florida for outdoor events, birthday parties, school carnivals, and community gatherings — and a thriving local party rental industry has grown to match. Whether you need an inflatable bounce house, a towering water slide, folding tables and chairs, or a popcorn machine, Tampa's party rental companies have you covered.

Most Popular Party Rentals in Tampa

Inflatables

Bounce houses and water slides remain the #1 requested party rental across Tampa Bay. Modern inflatables are made from commercial-grade PVC vinyl, built for safety, and can handle 4–8 kids at a time. Water slide combos are especially popular during Tampa's long summer season (March through October).

Tables and Chairs

Most rental companies offer 6-foot and 8-foot folding tables alongside white plastic or padded folding chairs. Packages that include 10 tables and 100 chairs are common for larger events. Linens are sometimes available as an add-on.

Concession Equipment

Popcorn machines, snow cone machines, and cotton candy machines are crowd-pleasers at any party. Most Tampa rental companies rent these by the day with supplies included or available separately.

Tents and Canopies

Tampa's afternoon thunderstorms are a reality — a 10x10 or 20x20 pop-up canopy provides shade and weather protection for outdoor events. Larger frame tents are available for weddings and corporate events.

How to Choose a Party Rental Company in Tampa

  • Check their insurance — any reputable company carries general liability insurance
  • Read Google reviews — look for consistent 4.5+ star ratings with specific comments about on-time delivery and clean equipment
  • Confirm their service area — most Tampa companies cover a 20–30 mile radius
  • Ask about their cancellation policy — Florida weather can be unpredictable
  • Verify they offer online booking — it's 2025, you shouldn't have to leave a voicemail to check availability

Party Rental Packages in Tampa

Many Tampa rental companies offer bundled packages that combine multiple items at a discount. A typical "birthday party package" might include a bounce house, a folding table, 10 chairs, and a concession machine for a flat rate. These packages can save 15–25% compared to renting each item separately.
