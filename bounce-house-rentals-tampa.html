What to Look for in a Tampa Bounce House Rental Company

Before you book, there are a few things every parent should check:

  • Insurance: Any legitimate bounce house rental company in Tampa should carry commercial general liability insurance. Ask for proof before signing a contract.
  • Cleanliness: Inflatables should be cleaned and inspected between every rental. Ask about their sanitation process.
  • Delivery and Setup: Most Tampa rental companies include free delivery and setup within a certain radius. Confirm whether your address qualifies.
  • Variety: A good rental company will offer more than just basic bounce houses — look for water slides, obstacle courses, and combo units.
